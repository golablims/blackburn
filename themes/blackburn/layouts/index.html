Solution Scope

Our project consisted of several components:

Understanding the problem: Conducting research to understand the complexities of the problem and create an identity for the solution, including a name and institutional image.

Developing a Platform App: Creating a centralized application with Slingr to manage business data, automate complex workflows, and integrate with notification systems through endpoints.

Developing a Mobile App: Developing a mobile application with Flutterflow to provide users with easy access to specific features.

Creating a Landing Page: Using Webflow to create a landing page for the LIMS company, aimed at attracting new patients, showcasing the company's expertise, generating leads, and establishing credibility.

Creating Documentation: Developing documentation to record the entire process and key points of the solution using HUGO, a static site generator.

Understanding the problem

We focused on creating a Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS), a software system designed to manage, track, and document laboratory processes, data, and samples. Our client, a fictitious testing laboratory called "golab" based in Mendoza, Argentina, aimed to earn the loyalty and satisfaction of their patients and become the laboratory that they rely on and recommend with confidence.

Branding

We created the name "golab" short for "go laboratory." We also designed a unique color palette that departs from the typical colors used in the healthcare industry to create an elegant and modern image. Our minimalist logos reinforce the brand's emphasis on elegance and modernity.

Features

Our LIMS platform includes several key features:

Data Set: A system for managing sample information.

Samples Management and Workflow: Workflow and process management to streamline laboratory operations.

Report Automation: Automatic generation of sample reports when sample statuses change to "ready" with patient information.

Customer Accessioning: Easy management of patient accessioning.

Automated Notifications: Notifications that automatically alert users to important changes in sample statuses and other key metrics.

Metrics Dashboard: A dashboard to quickly assess sample and patient statuses.
